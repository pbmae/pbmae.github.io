Hello World

My name is Phoebe. I make mathematical models that describe how we perceive the world! I like to refer to myself as a Vision Scientist in Training as I am currently working towards a PhD at York University in Canada. I received two MPhil degrees (Bioengineering, Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics) from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. I am originally from Manila, Philippines and speak fluent Tagalog. My colleagues say that I am skilled at editorial work (i.e. proofreading their application materials). In my free time, I like to draw, read fiction, and play chill games. I am at my happiest while working on a passion project.

Industry Collaborations

Stereoscopic Depth Perception User Study, Oct 2024-Present

  • We are examining how the "goodness of fit" of the VR-HMD's design to the user's anatomy affects shape perception. More soon!

Pupil Swim User Study, 2022-2023

  • We developed a psychophysical model for predictive testing of pupil swim in new VR-HMD designs.
  • Read our paper here: https://doi.org/10.1002/sdtp.16736

Machine-learning "sensors" for water quality, 2020-2022

  • A project with the Drainage Services Department of Hong Kong.
  • We predicted complex water quality parameters from simpler sensors.
  • Read our paper here: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.envres.2022.112953
  • Also, read our review paper based on my preparations for this project: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jwpe.2021.102367
